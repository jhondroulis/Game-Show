## Tips for Creating Questions

1. **Survey Style**: Frame questions as "Name something..." or "Name a..."
2. **Point Values**: Top answer gets most points (30-40), descending from there
3. **Answer Count**: 4-8 answers per question works best
4. **Aliases**: Include common variations, abbreviations, and synonyms
5. **Relevance**: Tailor questions to your team's work environment
6. **Difficulty**: Mix obvious and less-obvious answers
